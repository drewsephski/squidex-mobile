Security

Private by default

Your code never touches our servers. Squidex runs a local bridge on your machine — no cloud relay, no third-party access.

Local bridge

The bridge runs on your machine, on your network. Nothing goes through our servers. Your code stays where it belongs.

Encrypted connection

All communication between your phone and bridge is encrypted. Pair it with Tailscale for an additional layer of network security.

You control access

Configure auth tokens, restrict allowed commands, and run the bridge however you want. No defaults that compromise your machine.

Open source

Every line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, contribute to it. No black boxes between you and your machine.

Setup

Up and running in three steps

No cloud accounts, no subscriptions. Just your machine, your bridge, and your phone.

1

Install the bridge

Run a single command in your terminal to set up the private bridge on your development machine.

npx squidex init
2

Start the service

Launch the bridge on your local network or Tailscale. It auto-generates a secure connection URL.

squidex start
3

Pair your phone

Open Squidex, scan or enter your bridge URL, and start controlling your AI tools from anywhere.
