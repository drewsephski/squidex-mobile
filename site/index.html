How it works

Up and running in three steps

No cloud accounts, no subscriptions. Just your machine, your bridge, and your phone.

1

Install the bridge

Run a single command in your terminal to set up the private bridge on your development machine.

npx squidex init
2

Start the service

Launch the bridge on your local network or Tailscale. It auto-generates a secure connection URL.

squidex start
3

Pair your phone

Open Squidex, scan or enter your bridge URL, and start controlling your AI tools from anywhere.
