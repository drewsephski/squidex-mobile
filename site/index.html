04
Security

Private
by default.

Your code never touches our servers. Squidex runs a local bridge on your machine — no cloud relay, no third-party access.

Local bridge

Runs on your machine, on your network. Nothing touches our servers. Your code stays where it belongs.

Encrypted

All communication is encrypted end-to-end. Pair with Tailscale for an additional layer of network security.

You control access

Configure auth tokens, restrict commands, run the bridge however you want. No defaults that compromise.

Open source

Every line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, contribute. No black boxes between you and your machine.

06
Setup

Up and running
in three steps.

No cloud accounts, no subscriptions. Just your machine, your bridge, and your phone.

01

Install the bridge

Run a single command in your terminal to set up the private bridge on your development machine.

$ npx squidex init
02

Start the service

Launch the bridge on your local network or Tailscale. It auto-generates a secure connection URL.

$ squidex start
03

Pair your phone

Open Squidex, scan or enter your bridge URL, and start controlling your AI tools from anywhere on earth.
